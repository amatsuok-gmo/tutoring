Services

What we work on together.

Sessions are tailored to your level and goals. Whether you're preparing for an exam or simply want to hold a conversation, there's a path for you.

01
Conversational French

Build fluency through guided discussion on topics that interest you — film, food, current events, literature. No textbook required.

02
Academic writing

Essay structure, argumentation, and style in French. Ideal for university students and those preparing for DELF/DALF examinations.

03
Grammar & pronunciation

Targeted work on the mechanics that hold you back — verb moods, liaison, register — with clear explanations grounded in real usage.

04
Literature & culture

Read Camus, Senghor, or Duras with someone who studied them seriously. Perfect for learners who want French to feel like more than a skill.
